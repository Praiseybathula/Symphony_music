Guitar Chords

A chord is a group of three or more notes sounding simultaneously, though they can be also found in 'arpeggio' or 'broken' form, where the notes are played separately, yet still considered as part of a chord. Chords are present in many musical traditions of the world, and constitute an important branch of study in western music.
